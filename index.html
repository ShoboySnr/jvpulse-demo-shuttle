HOW To Get Started

  • Step 1: A Bolt Account is required. If you do not have, you can download here.
  • Step 2: Supply your email and phone number you used to sign up on Bolt.
  • Step 3: Supply your home location and office location addresses. Fixed cost Rides are Exclusively for travel from your home to your office. They are valid for trips taken Mondays to Fridays. Indicate your full locations below for us to calculate your fare.
  • Step 4: Click the Submit button in our form after filling the above details, and we will email you how much your subscription would cost and how to make payment.
  • Step 5: Once payment is received you will be added to The JV Pulse Bolt account and can enjoy rides to and from home to office.
